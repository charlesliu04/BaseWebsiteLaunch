1. Holy Bible

Before my time at Boston College, spirituality held little place in my life. However, engaging with the scriptures transformed my perspective, infusing my daily existence with newfound purpose and clarity. The wisdom of the Bible, with its rich tapestry of moral lessons, historical narratives, and timeless parables, encouraged me to adopt a more disciplined lifestyle, drawing me closer to the ideals exemplified by Christ. This profound shift was not merely about adhering to religious tenets but about embracing a way of life that prioritizes compassion, humility, and a deep-seated sense of inner peace. The stories and teachings of the Bible have become a cornerstone of my personal growth, shaping my values and guiding my actions in a world that often seems devoid of such depth.

2. How to Win Friends and Influence People By Dale Carnegie

One crucial skill that academia often overlooks is effective communication. Dale Carnegie's masterpiece is essential for anyone looking to excel in human relations. This book, another timeless gem, holds a special place as my favorite, offering invaluable insights into interpersonal success.

3. Can't Hurt me By David Goggins

David Goggins' story is a testament to the power of discipline and resilience. His teachings provide a blueprint for overcoming adversity and pushing beyond perceived limitations. This book is a must-read for those seeking to fortify their mental toughness.

4. Thinking Fast and Slow By Daniel Kahneman

Daniel Kahneman's Thinking, Fast and Slow offers groundbreaking insights into the dual-process theory of the mind. This book will challenge your understanding of how we think, make decisions, and navigate the complexities of the cognitive world. It's a treasure trove of wisdom that can alter your perception of reality.

5. All of Percy Jacson books and their spin offs By Rick Rioridan

Rick Riordan's Percy Jackson series, along with its spin-offs, has left an indelible mark on my childhood. These books not only entertained me but also instilled a sense of adventure and curiosity about the world and its myths. I wouldn't be who I am today without them.
